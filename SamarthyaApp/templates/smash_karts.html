Smash Karts

About the event

A 3D multiplayer kart-battle game is called Smash Karts.

To win, drive your go-kart, get some weapons, and blow-up other go-karts. Play on to level up, unlock new characters and win rewards.

There will be three rounds to the game.

The top 7 from the first round, top 3 from the second round, and top 1 from the third round will determine the winner.

Rules

Your primary objective is to gather power-ups and use them to eliminate your adversaries.

You must drive through the flying balloons to collect power-ups but be careful around gift parcels because those aren't the ones you want.
